Biblical Miracles
Bible Guide

Explore the miraculous works of God throughout the Bible. Click on any miracle to learn more about it.

Creation of the World

God spoke the universe into existence in six days, creating light, sky, land, plants, sun, moon, stars, sea creatures, birds, animals, and finally humans in His own image.

Genesis 1:1-2:3

Noah's Ark and the Flood

God sent a worldwide flood to cleanse the earth of wickedness, saving only Noah, his family, and representatives of each animal species in a massive ark.

Genesis 6-9

Parting of the Red Sea

Moses stretched out his hand over the Red Sea, and God caused a strong east wind to divide the waters, allowing the Israelites to cross on dry ground while drowning the pursuing Egyptian army.

Exodus 14:21-31

Manna from Heaven

God provided bread from heaven each morning to feed the Israelites during their 40 years in the wilderness, with double portions before the Sabbath.

Exodus 16:4-36

Jordan River Stopped

When the priests carrying the Ark of the Covenant stepped into the Jordan River at flood stage, the waters stopped flowing, allowing Israel to cross into the Promised Land on dry ground.

Joshua 3:14-17

Fall of Jericho

After marching around Jericho for seven days as God commanded, the Israelites' shouts and trumpet blasts caused the city walls to collapse, giving them victory.

Joshua 6:1-27

Elijah and the Fire from Heaven

In a contest with Baal's prophets, Elijah prayed and God sent fire from heaven to consume his water-soaked sacrifice, proving He is the one true God.

1 Kings 18:20-40

Widow's Oil Multiplied

Elisha told a poor widow to gather empty jars, and God miraculously multiplied her small amount of oil to fill them all, providing money to pay her debts and live on.

2 Kings 4:1-7

Fiery Furnace

When Shadrach, Meshach and Abednego refused to worship a golden image, God protected them in a blazing furnace so hot it killed their guards, leaving them unharmed.

Daniel 3:8-30

Daniel in the Lion's Den

God sent an angel to shut the mouths of lions when Daniel was thrown into their den for praying to God rather than the king, emerging unharmed the next morning.

Daniel 6:1-28

Jonah and the Great Fish

After Jonah was thrown overboard for disobeying God, a great fish swallowed him and three days later vomited him onto dry land, giving him a second chance to obey.

Jonah 1:17-2:10

Virgin Birth of Jesus

Jesus was miraculously conceived by the Holy Spirit in the virgin Mary, fulfilling prophecy and demonstrating God's power to bring salvation to humanity.

Matthew 1:18-25; Luke 1:26-38

Water Turned to Wine

At a wedding in Cana, Jesus turned six stone jars of water into the finest wine, His first public miracle that revealed His glory and power.

John 2:1-11

Feeding the 5,000

With just five loaves and two fish, Jesus miraculously fed over 5,000 people, with twelve baskets of leftovers remaining after all had eaten.

Matthew 14:13-21; John 6:1-14

Walking on Water

Jesus walked on the Sea of Galilee during a storm, inviting Peter to join Him and saving him when he began to sink from doubt.

Matthew 14:22-33

Raising Lazarus

After being dead four days, Lazarus was brought back to life when Jesus called him out of the tomb, demonstrating His power over death.

John 11:1-44

Resurrection of Jesus

After being crucified and buried for three days, Jesus rose from the dead, conquering sin and death and providing eternal life for all who believe.

Matthew 28:1-10; 1 Corinthians 15:3-8

Day of Pentecost

The Holy Spirit descended on believers with tongues of fire, enabling them to speak in other languages and launching the church's mission to the world.

Acts 2:1-41

Paul's Conversion

Jesus appeared in a blinding light to Saul (Paul) on the road to Damascus, miraculously transforming this persecutor of Christians into a devoted apostle.

Acts 9:1-19

About Our Church

Arena of Grace and Testimonies is a dynamic, Spirit-filled church committed to making disciples of all nations. Founded by Pastor Martin Osei Mensah, we've grown from a small Bible study group to a thriving congregation impacting our city and beyond.

Our vision is to create a community where people encounter God's grace, experience life-changing testimonies, and are equipped to fulfill their divine purpose. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit.

Whether you're exploring faith or looking to deepen your relationship with Christ, you'll find a welcoming family here at Arena of Grace.
